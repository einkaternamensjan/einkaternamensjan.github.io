# The Bird in the Window 

The bird in the window sits there and broods

The bird in the window springs from the flames

The bird in the window feels right at home 

The bird in the window is utterly alone 

The bird in the window twitters and sings 

The bird in the window suffers and seethes

The bird in the window searches for a bride 

The bird in the window was cruelly denied 

The bird in the window watches you sleep 

The bird in the window watches you creep 

The bird in the window holds all things secret 

The bird in the window cannot forget it 

The bird in the window fears the rage

The bird in the window drowns in your blood 

The bird in the window taps into the power 

The bird in the window keeps you from the door 

The bird in the window pecks and bites

The bird in the window strikes and fights

The bird in the window cannot endure 

The bird in the window is no simple boor

Composed yet corrupted, the bird at your side

Shattered and alone, into death you both slide.
