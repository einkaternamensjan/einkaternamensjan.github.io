#The Trap of Reality

A person's moral level can only be confirmed by urging them to action. Behind the mirror of the most vile vanity — in which man indulges his ostentatious, superior values — hides the dreadful face of the real. The self-satisfaction of one who deludes himself about his own superiority comes swiftly to an end the moment he stumbles into the trap of reality. His strict morals suddenly turn to fear and cowardice. He can only slither away and lick the wounds of his ego
